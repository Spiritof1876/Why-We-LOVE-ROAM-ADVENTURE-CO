Why We LOVE ROAM ADVENTURE CO

Roam Adventure Co. is definitely one of the newer brands but has come on strong over the last few years as one of the top players. Let's give you a quick intro to their tent line and share with you a few differentiators. The Vagabond is their signature 4 season tent that mounts to most racks with the included hardware. The Vagabond comes in 3 versions, the Standard (56") which is good for two, the XL (72") which is perfect for 4 and a Lite (48"), which believe it or not the lightest, smallest, quickest and most affordable in the family, perfect for 1 or 2 campers.

All versions are made of rugged polycotton ripstop fabric with a robust diamond weaved nylon rainfly. We love Roam not only for their rad colorways (BEST IN THE GAME), but their thoughtful features, included accessories, and rugged design.

ROAM is one of the few companies that offer dual skylights with a view through the rainfly and functional windows for stargazing and airflow.

This tent also benefits from one of the most comfortable mattresses available in a softshell. The mattress features a waterproof bottom with a zipper cover, as well as an included condensation mat for extra cushion, but also to help reduce moisture underneath your mattress.

This tent also features a rugged diamond plated insulated floor, with an exterior gear locker for extra storage. These may sound like standard features, but everything from their unique signature windows, to the included bootbags and gear locker, as well as the mattress and diamond plated floor, these are all features only found standard in ROAM tents.

Lastly, ROAM offers one the slimmest profiles for a softshell, and an exterior cover that we think is miles ahead of the competition. It features a unique buckle system vs the typical strap and D ring. All the covers are standard Black with a nice ROAM patch however all versions come in 3 amazing colors; Slate w/ Navy accents, Forrest w/ Orange accents, and Black on Black. Overall, it's the colorways, the quality, the included upgrades and FREE Shipping that make ROAM one of the hottest soft shells in 2022.
